Welcome to Yuva Samarpan

Yuva Samarpan is dedicated to transforming lives through education, healthcare, and social welfare. Our journey began with a simple mission—to ensure every child, woman, and elderly person has access to basic necessities and opportunities to thrive.

At Yuva Samarpan, we believe in creating a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Through our core initiatives, we provide quality education to underprivileged children, ensuring they have the tools to build a better future. Our healthcare programs offer free medical aid, check-ups, and life-saving treatments to those in need. We stand by the elderly, offering them shelter, care, and dignity in their golden years. Orphaned and abandoned children find a safe haven with us, receiving love, education, and a chance at a brighter tomorrow. We empower women through skill development and legal support, helping them become independent and confident members of society. Additionally, our sports and youth development programs encourage discipline, teamwork, and leadership, giving young minds the chance to excel beyond their circumstances. Together, with your support, we can transform lives and spread hope where it’s needed the most.

Our Mission

At Yuva Samarpan, our mission is to uplift communities by addressing fundamental challenges in education, healthcare, and social empowerment. We believe that every individual, regardless of their background, deserves access to opportunities that can transform their lives. Through our education programs, we provide underprivileged children with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to build a better future. Our healthcare initiatives ensure that essential medical assistance, check-ups, and treatments reach those who lack access to proper medical care. Beyond education and healthcare, we work towards empowering marginalized communities, especially women and the elderly, by fostering financial independence, legal awareness, and emotional support. Our goal is to bridge the gap between privilege and poverty, creating a society where compassion, equality, and empowerment lead to sustainable growth and a brighter future for all.

Our Work

Through various programs, we provide underprivileged children access to education, empower women with skill development, and offer essential healthcare services. Our initiatives focus on holistic development, creating self-sustaining communities.
